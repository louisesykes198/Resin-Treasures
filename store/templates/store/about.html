About Resin Treasures

Welcome to Resin Treasures — a one-woman creative studio where handmade resin art comes to life. Every piece you’ll find here is designed, poured, and polished with care from a small home studio in the UK.

Resin Treasures is a new and growing passion project, built from a love of crafting and a desire to create beautiful, meaningful keepsakes. As a solo maker, I handle everything — from design and production to packing your orders. It’s a true labour of love, and your support means everything.

What I Make

I specialize in:

  • • Handmade jewellery – earrings and necklaces
  • • Unique keyrings for all occasions
  • • Thoughtful gifts for teachers and new parents
  • • Custom animal and reptile-themed décor
  • • Resin wall art and festive holiday decorations

Why Resin?

Resin allows for incredible creativity — from shimmering finishes to embedded details like glitter, flowers, or custom colour blends. No two pieces are ever alike, which makes each item from Resin Treasures truly one-of-a-kind.
