Returns & Refunds

We want you to be delighted with your purchase! If you’re not completely satisfied, here’s how you can return or exchange your items.

Eligibility

  • Items must be returned within 14 days of receipt and in their original condition.
  • Sale items cannot be returned.
  • Personalised items can only be returned if they arrive damaged or broken.
  • Items that have been used, worn, or altered cannot be returned.

Return Process

  1. Contact us at resintreasures5@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return.
  2. We will provide instructions for returning your item, including our returns address.
  3. Ship the item back to us securely. We recommend using a tracked service.

Refunds

  • Refunds will be issued to your original payment method once the item is received and inspected.
  • Refunds are processed within 7 working days of receiving the returned item.

Exchanges

  • We are happy to exchange items for a different size, colour, or product.
  • Please contact us first to confirm availability before returning your item.
  • Exchanges are free of charge for faulty items; customers cover shipping for other exchanges.

Faulty or Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged or faulty, please contact us within 48 hours with a photo of the damage. We will provide a replacement or refund free of charge.

Shipping Costs for Returns

  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is faulty or damaged.
  • We recommend using a tracked service to ensure your item arrives safely.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about returns or refunds, please email us at resintreasures5@gmail.com.
