Help & FAQs

Here you’ll find answers to some of the most common questions about Resin Treasures. If you can’t find what you’re looking for, please contact us.

  • How do I place an order? – Browse our shop, select your items, add to basket, and checkout using your preferred payment method.
  • Can I change my order after placing it? – Unfortunately, once an order is submitted we cannot guarantee changes. Please contact us immediately if you need adjustments.
  • Do you offer gift wrapping? – Yes! You can select gift wrapping at checkout for an additional fee.

  • What delivery options are available? – See our Delivery Info page for full details and costs.
  • How long will delivery take? – Standard delivery usually takes 2–5 business days. You will receive tracking updates.
  • Can I track my order? – Yes, we provide tracking information for all deliveries via email.

  • What payment methods do you accept? – We accept major debit/credit cards and PayPal.
  • Is my payment information safe? – Yes, all payments are processed securely via Stripe/PayPal.

  • How do I return an item? – See our Returns & Refunds page for step-by-step instructions.
  • Can I return personalised items? – Only if they arrive broken or damaged.
  • How long do refunds take? – Refunds are usually processed within 7 working days after we receive the returned item.

  • Do you ship internationally? – Yes, please contact us for shipping costs to your country.
  • Can I cancel my subscription? – Yes, unsubscribe at any time from our newsletters link.
  • How can I contact customer service? – Email resintreasures5@gmail.com or use the contact form.
